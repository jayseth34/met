Metalline is based in Bengaluru, southern part of India. Specialized in processing of stainless steel flat products in cutting, slitting and polishing to various finishes like No.4, Hairline, Scotch Brite, No.8, Bead Blast, Vibration, Etched and PVD Coated.

Metalline is one of the best stainless steel flat product service centre in the world with a long and successful history in Processing, Import, Export and Trading, established since 1959 as a family venture. Company has three processing centres and another one coming soon, followed by five distribution centres across India. Metalline is considered to be the best supplier for providing stainless steel for use in construction of Strategic industrial, Infrastructural and Residential Projects providing our customers with high-quality products and services.

Metalline operates in every sector where stainless steel is used from architecture to construction, elevator industry, interior designs, urban design, aviation and automotive, defining the highest quality standards for the global market.
